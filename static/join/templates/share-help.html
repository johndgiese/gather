Inviting Players

Each game of Gather has a "join link". Your friends can join the game simply by click this link.

There are many ways to share links. Most browsers have a "share" button, which will allow you to send your contacts the link without having to type it in (the share button is a square with an arrow coming out). On Android, the share button has three little circles connected by two lines, or it is a circle with two arrows coming out of it.

As a last resort, you can simply ask your friends to type the link directly into their browser.
