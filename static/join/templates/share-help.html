Inviting Players

Each game of Gather has a "join link". Your friends can join the game by visiting this link.

Most browsers have a "share" button, which will allow you to send your contacts the link easily. On iOS the share button is a square with an arrow coming out. On Android, the share button either has three little circles connected by two lines or is a circle with two arrows coming out of it.

As a last resort, you can simply ask your friends to type the link directly into their browser.
