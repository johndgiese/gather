How do you play?

1. Create Game

Visit our website or download an app for iOS or android*. You can then create a game. Your first game is free, and you get a free game for each friend you invite. Once you buy the game you can play for free forever.

2. Invite Friends

Share a link to your game via text, Twitter, or Facebook. Your friends can join by clicking the link. No need to download an app or create an account.

3. Play

Each round, one player reads a prompt to the group. Every player picks a response from their hand. Then the prompter reads aloud all the responses, and everyone votes on their favorite.

Why use phones?

Never run out cards

If we printed our words as a deck of cards, you would have to carry it to your friends house in a bigger, blacker suitcase. And expansion cards are always being added, at no additional cost.

Inside Jokes

Customize the play deck. Football, sororities, celebrities, Putin. If you don’t like our decks, create your own! Then share it with your friends.* Play with your dirty friends, and your mom.

Play anywhere

Play at the pub, standing in line at the movies, waiting at the airport, or on a road trip. No need to bring a deck, or worry about spilling on it.
