How do you play?

1. Create game

Visit our website. Create an account. Then start a game, selecting which card decks you want to use*.

2. Invite friends

Share a link to your game via text, Twitter, or Facebook. Your friends can join your game by just clicking the link, without downloading an app or even creating an account!

3. Play

Each round, one player reads a prompt to the group. Every player picks a response from their hand. Then the prompter reads aloud all the responses, and everyone votes on their favorite.

Why use phones?

Never run out of cards

If we printed our words as a deck of cards, you would have to carry it to your friends house in a bigger, blacker suitcase. And expansion cards are always being added.

Inside jokes

Customize the play deck. Football, sororities, celebrities, Putin. Play with your dirty friends, and your mom.
