Gather is a comical group word game
for friends to play when they're together.

1. Create game

Visit our website. Create an account. Then start a game, selecting which card decks you want to use.

2. Invite friends

Your friends can join your game by just clicking it's special "join link"; they won't have to download an app or even create an account!

3. Play

Each round, one player reads a prompt to the group. Every player picks a response from their hand. Then everyone votes on their favorite responses.
